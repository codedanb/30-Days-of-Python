# UIUX_01_BASICS

## Introduction to User Interface and Experience Design

*Target Audience*: Absolute beginner (Grade school student / Non-technical person).  
*Philosophy*: Assume NO prior knowledge. Explain "what" and "why" before "how".

### What is UI/UX?

**UI (User Interface)**  
The look and feel of a product.

**UX (User Experience)**  
How users feel when using it.

**Why UI/UX?**  
To make products easy and enjoyable to use.

### Basic Elements

**Colors and Fonts**  
Visual building blocks.

**Buttons and Links**  
Interactive elements.

**Layout**  
Arranging elements on screen.

### User-Centered Design

**Users First**  
Thinking about what users need.

**Simplicity**  
Keeping things easy.

**Consistency**  
Making things predictable.

### Analogy  
UI/UX is like designing a playground: fun, safe, and easy to play on.
