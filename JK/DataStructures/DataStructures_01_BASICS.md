**What are Data Structures?**  
Data structures are ways to organize data, like arranging toys in boxes or on shelves for easy access.

**Why Data Structures?**  
They make storing and finding information fast and efficient.

**How to start?**  
Use lists in code or draw diagrams.

**Fundamental Concepts**

- **Lists**: Flexible collections.
