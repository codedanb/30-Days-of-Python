## Research Frontiers in System Design

*Target Audience*: PhD Researcher / Academic.  
*Philosophy*: Explore theoretical foundations, emerging paradigms, and research frontiers.

### Theoretical Foundations

- Formal methods in system design.  
- Model checking for distributed systems.  
- Game theory in resource allocation.

### Emerging Paradigms

- Self-healing and self-organizing systems.  
- Quantum computing architectures.  
- Neuromorphic system designs.

### Research Frontiers

- AI-driven system optimization.  
- Blockchain-based system architectures.  
- Bio-inspired computing systems.

### Advanced Topics

- Causal consistency models.  
- Byzantine fault tolerance advances.  
- Energy-efficient system designs.

### Interdisciplinary Applications

- Systems design in IoT ecosystems.  
- Cognitive architectures.  
- Social system modeling.

### Next Research Directions  
Explore autonomous system design or quantum-resistant architectures.
